MOXIE's Analysis

March 2026 was an outstanding month for CMW Inc. across every channel. The website drew 14,011 sessions — a strong indicator of growing brand awareness. Paid advertising delivered 544,618 total impressions and 13,807 clicks across seven platforms. Google Search led the way with a 10.87% CTR on 26,500 impressions, well above the industry average. TikTok Ads generated massive reach with 253,963 impressions and 1,811 clicks. Social media was equally impressive with 69,756 impressions, 114,760 views, and 8,439 likes — building significant brand engagement. Email newsletters reached 4,589 contacts with an 85% open rate and 983 clicks — exceptional deliverability and engagement. GeoFencing, Microsoft Ads, and Google Display all contributed strong performance rounding out a fully diversified marketing presence.
