# MOXIE AI Insights — Client Explainer Video Plan

## Overview
A 2-3 minute screen-recording explainer video that walks clients through how to use their MOXIE AI Insights dashboard. Friendly, upbeat tone with MOXIE as the guide.

---

## Video Specs
- **Length**: 2:00 - 3:00
- **Format**: Screen recording with voiceover
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (16:9)
- **Style**: Clean screen capture with subtle zoom-ins on key areas, cursor highlights
- **Music**: Light, upbeat background track (low volume)
- **Voiceover**: Friendly, professional (could use AI voice or record Meg/Todd)

---

## Script & Storyboard

### Scene 1: Welcome (0:00 - 0:15)
**Screen**: Landing page with MOXIE mascot, login fields
**Voiceover**:
> "Welcome to MOXIE AI Insights — your monthly marketing performance dashboard, powered by Inbound Blend. Let me show you how to access and explore your reports."

**Action**: Show the login page with MOXIE mascot front and center

---

### Scene 2: Logging In (0:15 - 0:30)
**Screen**: Login page, typing in client number and access code
**Voiceover**:
> "To get started, enter your 7-digit client number and your access code, then click View My Dashboard. Your client number and access code were provided by your Inbound Blend team — if you need them, just reach out to Meg."

### Scene 3: Your Dashboard (0:30 - 1:00)
**Screen**: Client dashboard page
**Voiceover**:

**Action**:
- Zoom into the Key Trends card, highlight the green ↑ badges
- Pan across the MOXIE mascot and branding

**Voiceover** (continued):
> "On the right side, you have quick-access buttons. Meet with Meg lets you schedule a call with your account manager directly through Calendly. Dropbox takes you to your shared project folder with all your assets. And Legacy Reports opens your historical data in Google Sheets."

**Action**:
- Highlight each button as it's mentioned
- Quick click on "Meet w/ Meg" to show Calendly opening in new tab (then close)

---

## Production Notes

### Recording Tips
- Use a clean browser with no bookmarks bar, extensions, or personal tabs visible
- Use a demo client account (e.g., MedSearch 2009003 or Bart Inman Air 1908001 — they have the most complete data)
- Record at 1920x1080, browser at ~90% zoom for clean capture
- Use a tool like Loom, ScreenFlow, or OBS for recording
- Add subtle cursor highlighting (yellow circle around cursor clicks)

### Voiceover Options
1. **Meg records it** — most personal, clients know her voice
2. **AI voiceover** — use ElevenLabs or similar for consistent, professional tone
3. **Todd records it** — good for the intro, Meg takes over for the walkthrough

### Post-Production
- Add lower-third labels for each section as it appears
- Add subtle zoom animations (105-110% zoom on key areas)
- Add a light background music track
- Add Inbound Blend branded intro/outro cards
- Export as MP4, upload to Vimeo for embedding

### Distribution
- Embed on each client's dashboard page (optional "Watch Tutorial" button)
- Email to clients with their login credentials
- Post in each client's Slack channel
- Add to Inbound Blend website

### Estimated Timeline
- Script review: 1 day
- Screen recording: 1-2 hours
- Voiceover recording: 30 minutes
- Post-production editing: 2-3 hours
- Total: 1-2 business days
